# LEVEL 1 CLASS RESTRICTION GUIDE
# Beginner-Friendly Fantasy RPG Ruleset
# Scope:
- Level 1 only
- No multiclassing
- No feats
- No subclass mechanics
- No advanced reactions
- No magical artifacts

---

# GLOBAL LEVEL 1 RULES

## Ability Score Limits

| Rule | Value |
|---|---|
| Minimum Ability Score | 8 |
| Maximum Ability Score | 17 |
| Recommended Total Pool | 72–75 |
